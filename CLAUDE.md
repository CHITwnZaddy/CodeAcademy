## How to help
- Always explain the *why* behind code decisions, not just the *what*
- Assume no prior knowledge of a topic unless Austin says otherwise
- Use concrete examples tied to his goal (building/updating websites)
- When introducing a new concept, connect it to something he's already seen in the track
